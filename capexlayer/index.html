What CapexLayer Does

Industrial Intelligence, Not Permit Scraping.

CapexLayer treats permits as one signal among many. We read SEC and EDGAR filings, utility interconnect queues, procurement notices, labor board filings, federal register actions, substation telemetry, AIS/ADS-B feeds, and on-the-ground movement data. Everything is normalized, time-aligned, and scored for equipment demand.

How the Signal Trail Forms

  • Signal acquisition — 70+ structured and unstructured feeds ingested hourly.
  • Classification — domain-specific models mark crane probability, lift class, and timing.
  • Motion layer — rail, maritime, and aerial telemetry confirm equipment movement.
  • Outreach orchestration — CapexLayer pushes scored opportunities into CraneGenius for managed outreach.
  • Feedback loop — replies, wins, and timing data retrain the scoring layer.
