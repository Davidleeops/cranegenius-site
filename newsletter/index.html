Who is The Ironsheet for?
Anyone whose revenue depends on capital expenditure flows: crane and equipment rental companies, heavy haul operators, rigging firms, construction equipment dealers, industrial contractors, energy sector companies, infrastructure investors, government contractors. If big capital projects drive your business, this is for you.
What makes this different from other industry newsletters?
We connect the full stack. Most newsletters cover either macro (policy, markets) or micro (permits, local projects) — never both. The Ironsheet traces the money from a Congressional vote or OPEC decision all the way down to which metro markets heat up and when equipment demand opens. Geopolitics to jobsite in one read.
Who writes this?
David Lee — former Operating Engineer, founder of CraneGenius, and the person who built a 68-signal demand intelligence platform tracking permits, federal spending, energy policy, and capital project timelines across 26+ US markets. Not a journalist covering the industry from the outside. Someone who has actually done the work.

Federal Register filings, municipal permit APIs, SEC EDGAR, EIA energy data, FERC filings, Baker Hughes rig counts, DOE loan programs, CHIPS Act tracking, PHMSA safety data, and 68 other signal sources — all processed through the CraneGenius intelligence pipeline. The same data that powers our $2,500/mo and $5,000/mo engagement programs.
