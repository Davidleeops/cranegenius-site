# CTO Conversation Prep Template
Fill this before major CTO or senior engineer conversations.

1. **System Overview (60 seconds)** – Plain-English description of product surfaces and current posture.
2. **Critical Paths & Ownership** – List top workflows (financial data, platform build, datasets, intake, monitoring) with owners.
3. **Top 5 Risks Today** – Bullet points with evidence (file names, manifests, ADRs).
4. **Mitigation Status** – For each risk, note current mitigation and remaining unknowns.
5. **Recent Improvements** – Highlight completed actions tied to the hardening roadmap.
6. **Upcoming Commitments** – Next three deliverables with dates.
7. **Open Questions / Escalations** – Items requiring CTO guidance or external expertise.
8. **Data Freshness Evidence** – Timestamp or validation proof for `/data/financials.json`, `cranegenius_*`, Supabase feeds.
